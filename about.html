Who we are

Established in the year 2017, Chaurus Group has now grown into a cluster of specialized companies operating in the Engineering, Procurement, Construction, Trading, Man-power supply, Agricultural & other services.

The group is currently operating in following two Sectors at present.

  • Engineering, Procurement, Construction & Trading
  • Export Import – Agro Products



In order to accomplish our goals, we have developed the strategy which can be summarized in the following key points.

1) Availability of highly professional, motivated and experienced staff.
2) The use of latest technology and innovation for sustainable growth.
3) Flexible approach to our clients and availability at any time.

Engineering, Procurement, Construction & Trading

Under the company name Chaurus Services Pvt. Ltd. (CSPL) operating in EPC (Engineering, Procurement and Construction), Manpower Supply, Trading with wide range of services which expands Design, Engineering, Material Procurement, Skilled Manpower Supply and Project Management in the field of Mechanical, Piping, Pipeline, Electrical, Instrumentation, Civil, Structural of projects for Oil and Gas, Chemical , Power Sectors, Process Industries, Petrochemical Industries and Water Industry.

Export-Import – Agro Products

Under the company name Chaurus Exim operating in an Farming/Export company in India and is a leading exporter of Vegetables, Fruits, Spices, Grains and Pulses. We are renowned traders in the domestic markets and spreading our wings now to the international opportunities. Our business model is based on product proficiency in agricultural markets while also expanding to other markets aggressively. We wish to contribute to societies that enjoy enhanced livelihood as a result of fair and ethical growing and trade in fresh produce. In order to turn our vision into a viable reality and encourage a healthy food environment on a global level, we intend to expand our services globally and take an active part in this exciting agriculture revolution.

CSPL Engineering Company culture is identified by six core values:

1. Commitment: We are committed to our partners, customers and suppliers.

3. Teamwork: We work together as a unit.

5. Ethics: We are professional and transparent in our service
