Who we are

Chaurus Services Pvt. Ltd.(CSPL) is established in 2017 and has been activated in the field of Engineering, Procurement and Construction, Manpower Supply, Trading.

Our range of services include Design, Engineering, Material Procurement, Skilled Manpower Supply and Project Management in the field of Mechanical, Piping, Pipeline, Electrical, Instrumentation, Civil, Structural of projects for Oil and Gas, Chemical , Power Sectors, Process Industries, Petrochemical Industries and Water Industry.

Gradually, the activities approached for the Oil and Gas, Chemical, Power Sectors, Process Industries, Petrochemical Industries and Water Industry and project association with leader foreign companies. Now CSPL is qualified company in project management, engineering, procurement, construction and manpower supply, not only for oil, gas, and petrochemical industries, but also for other industries such as power and chemical plants.

CSPL has proceeded and built a proven record of success and reliability because it has always dedicated itself to helping its clients to meet their total goals. Our experiences are now allowing us to make a good partner for clients and foreign engineering and construction contractors to respond to this need.

Our Vision

Vision

To become a leading Engineering, Procurement, Construction, manpower Supply Company in oil & gas & other industries committed to quality and timely completion of projects, continuously enhancing customer satisfaction & believing in the aspect of “Together for Growth”.
